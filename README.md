A Mod That Adds Plants From PVZ to the game. Also has a plant food mechanic that applies a buff that only works on plants and does different things depending on the plant. Planned Updates: Definitely new plants, and maybe pvz boss bloons! There is a bug with teh plant food where sometimes it does nothing the first time it is applied hopefully that will be fixed soon as well.
